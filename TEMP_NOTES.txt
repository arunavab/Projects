Use Cases of ML in Insurance Domain:
Risk Modeling: Analyze claims to predict Profit or Loss. 
Demand Modeling: Predicts Demand for Insurance Product in the future. 
Detecting Fraud: Pretty obvious....
Processing Claims: Automate Claim Reporting and Processing.
Underwriting: Help underwriters analyze data collected from applicants to discover any inconsistences or raise red flags the underwriters might otherwise miss.
